ascend educational fund (AEF) gala

april 14, 2018

last month i had the amazing opportunity to give a short speech at the ascend educational fund (AEF) 2018 gala. AEF is an nyc scholarship aimed at helping immigrant students and children of immigrants help pay for college and provide them with mentorship to succeed in higher education. the gala was for fundraising event for next year's class of scholars.

i talked a little bit about my journey being an immigrant from Mexico and making it through to get into college as well as how the scholarship gave me a sense of community and helped make school affordable. here's some photos!

i had a good time getting to know everyone who attended the gala, thank you to everyone who came!
